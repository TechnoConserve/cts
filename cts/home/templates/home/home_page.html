About Conservation Technology Solutions Get to know us

CTS was founded to address the needs of those involved in natural resource conservation and help them leverage the power of both existing and upcoming technologies so they can do their jobs more quickly, more efficiently, and more easily.

We’re interested in developing tools that can help land managers, native seed producers, native seed collectors, and citizen scientists communicate more effectively and track the key elements that matter to them. If you’re involved in natural resource conservation efforts, please get in touch and share your ideas for how we can better assist you.

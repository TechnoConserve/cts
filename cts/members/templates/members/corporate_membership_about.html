About Corporate Membership

Conservation Technology Solutions Inc has a corporate membership program designed to give a voice to organizations interested in supporting and sponsoring CTS's mission.

Corporate members contribute unrestricted funds that are used to fund CTS priorities, which include, but are not limited to:

  • Funding the development of software to be used by biologists, ecologists, botanists, or other scientists involved in natural resource conservation.
  • Funding small, local citizen science projects designed to encourage people to think scientifically about the world around them.
  • Funding continuing operations and other miscellaneous expenses.

There are several levels of corporate membership: Diamond, Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Memberships are renewed annually.

Why join?

Current members may decide to join CTS for a variety of reasons, including:

  • A desire to give back to the community.
  • In recognition of a growing need to protect our natural environment.
  • Investing in and supporting the work of natural resource scientists.
  • Technical support and natural resource consulting services.
  • Tax deductions: Conservation Technology Solutions Inc is a 501(c)(3) non-profit charitable organization. Membership dues are tax-deductible for U.S. organizations; members outside of the United States should consult a tax advisor.

How do I become a member?

Fill out our onboarding form and we'll follow up with an invoice after your organization is approved by our board.

Benefits

Corporate members receive:

  • A single vote in votes put to membership.
  • An organization description on the Corporate Members page.
  • Your logo and link on the fundraising page.
  • A CTS Donor badge denoting your level of membership, for use on your website & elsewhere.
  • An annual impact report from the Board, featuring highlights from our work.
  • Announcement and thanks for your membership on our Twitter account (@ctsadmin)
